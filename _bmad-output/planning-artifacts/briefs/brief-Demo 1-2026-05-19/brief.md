# Product Brief: AI CareerOS

## Executive Summary

AI CareerOS is a personalized AI resume and career coaching platform that makes career development genuinely individualized. Unlike existing tools that produce generic, templated output regardless of who uses them, AI CareerOS learns each user's unique background — industry, role level, target companies, career stage — and delivers tailored resumes, interview prep, salary strategy, and career pathing.

The product uses a **B2B-first business model**: employers and universities pay, while individual job seekers get a generous free tier. This flips the current market where predatory billing and low-quality automation dominate. The AI career coach market is $6.69B (2026) growing at 22% CAGR, and user satisfaction with existing tools is remarkably low — Resume.io holds a 1.3/5 rating on Product Hunt with 92% one-star reviews.

## The Problem

**For job seekers:** Existing AI resume tools produce generic, often factually wrong output. Teal's AI has been documented as inserting legal requirements from job descriptions into resumes as "professional skills." Kickresume hits users with AI usage caps mid-cycle without warning. MyPerfectResume charged one user $100 instead of $2.95. The billing model across the industry is predatory: $1.95-2.95 trials that auto-renew at $24-30/month with notoriously difficult cancellation.

Beyond resumes, career coaching is nearly absent — less than 1% of AI users use it for career guidance. Job seekers navigating career changes, industry pivots, or first-time searches are left with expensive human coaches ($150-300/hr) or generic ChatGPT advice that doesn't understand their specific situation.

**For employers and universities:** [ASSUMPTION: Target customers] Companies spend $100B+ annually on training in the US alone but have no way to track career readiness at scale. University career centers operate at 1:1,000+ counselor-to-student ratios with fragmented tools. Neither has a unified platform connecting career development to measurable outcomes.

**Three user groups:**
- **Career switchers** (highest willingness to pay, most underserved)
- **Active job seekers** (need tailoring + tracking + interview prep)
- **Students & new grads** (price-sensitive, AI-native, future B2B pipeline)
- Employers and universities are the paying buyers.

## The Solution

AI CareerOS combines three layers:

**1. Personalized AI Resume Builder** — Not a template with a chatbot, but an AI that understands the user's industry, role history, target companies, and career trajectory. It coaches on framing, quantifies achievements, and optimizes for specific job descriptions without hallucinating credentials.

**2. AI Career Coach** — The real product behind the resume hook: personalized career pathing, skill gap analysis, mock interviews with company-specific feedback, salary negotiation practice, and LinkedIn optimization. [ASSUMPTION: Feature set] The AI builds a deep profile and delivers guidance that applies to the user's situation.

**3. B2B Platform** — Employer dashboard (candidate readiness scores, skill analytics) and university white-label platform (career center automation, outcome data). Placement rates, salary benchmarks, and skill progression prove value.

## What Makes This Different

| Dimension | Existing Tools | AI CareerOS |
|-----------|---------------|-------------|
| Personalization | Generic AI, same output for everyone | Deep profile, industry/role-aware coaching |
| Pricing | Predatory trials → $24-30/mo auto-renew | Free tier, simple $12/mo Pro, B2B revenue |
| Business model | B2C only, high churn (12-18%/mo) | B2B-first funds the free tier |
| Career coaching | <1% of usage | Core product behind the resume hook |
| Trust | AI hallucinates, billing complaints | Privacy-first, hybrid AI + human premium |
| Market position | Crowded, no dominant player | Unique B2B-first position in fragmented market |

## Success Criteria

- Users complete career coaching sessions, not just resume builds
- Free → Pro conversion rate validates willingness to pay
- Employer pilots confirm B2B pricing at $500-1,000/mo
- University pilots confirm $30-50K/yr institutional willingness to pay

**V1 scope:** AI resume builder, basic career coaching engine, and a read-only employer pilot dashboard. Everything else comes in V2+.

## Vision

In 2-3 years, AI CareerOS becomes the operating system for career development — from first internship search through executive transitions. The resume builder is the entry point; the coaching engine is the moat. By building the deepest personalization model in the market, AI CareerOS graduates from "resume tool" to "career OS" that users trust with every career decision.
